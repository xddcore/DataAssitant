V1.2 软件使用说明:
1.双击打开DataAssistant.exe
2.按照软件上的使用说明进行操作
3.禁止修改config.ini文件
4.如遇软件无法打开运行，请检查软件根目录下，是否包含必要文件:
1.sipeed.ico;2.config.ini.
5.VoTT下载地址:
https://github.com/microsoft/VoTT/releases/tag/v2.1.0
6.VoTT帮助文档:
https://github.com/Microsoft/VoTT/blob/master/README.md
7.目标检测(自动标注)图片收集说明:
1.您可以使用Data_Collection_Assitant(ObjectDection).py进行图片收集。
2.假如图片来自于互联网，请保证图片命名顺序为0.jpg,1.jpg,2.jpg...1000.jpg。为了保证自动标注的准确性，
还应保证上一张图片与下一张图片中的目标位置不发生巨大改变。

6.VoTT Help documentation:

7. Target detection (automatic annotation) picture collection instructions:
    1. You can use Data_Collection_Assitant (ObjectDection) .py to collect pictures.
    2. If the pictures come from the Internet, please ensure that the naming order of the pictures is 0.jpg, 1.jpg, 2.jpg ... 1000.jpg. 
        In order to ensure the accuracy of automatic labeling,It should also ensure that the target position in the previous picture and 
        the next picture does not change significantly
